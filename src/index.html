A Note From Bill

Hi, I'm Bill.

I help men over 40 get unstuck, rebuild momentum, and create an epic second act.

Maybe you've been hit with a divorce, job loss, health scare, or some other setback you didn't see coming.

Maybe life's constant challenges have just worn you down a little at a time.

Maybe to the outside world you are a success but inside you know you have more to achieve.

If any of that sounds familiar, you're in the right place.

I'm more than a coach and mentor. I'm someone who has lived through the kind of midlife gut punch that forces you to face hard truths and make real changes.

I know what it's like to lose your path and have to navigate through rugged, unfamiliar terrain to find a better one.

That's what Reboot Camp is about: helping men over 40 regain their edge, reclaim their direction, and build the best years of their lives.

Not through affirmations, manifesting or vision boards — but by putting in the work and taking bold action.
