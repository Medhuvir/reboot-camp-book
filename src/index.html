A note from our founder and head coach

Hi, I'm Bill McGlone.

I help men over 40 get unstuck, rebuild momentum, and create their epic second act.

Maybe you've been hit with a major life event like divorce, job loss or some other setback you didn't see coming.

Or maybe life's daily grind has worn you down a little at a time.

Maybe to the outside world you look successful, but inside, you know you have so much more to give.

If any of that sounds familiar, you're in the right place.

As a coach and mentor, I will be equal parts partner, instructor and respectful challenger of limits. I'm someone who has lived through the kind of midlife gut punch that forces you to face hard truths and make real changes.

I know what it's like to lose your path and have to navigate through rugged, unfamiliar terrain to find a better one.

That's what Reboot Camp is about: helping men over 40 regain their edge, reclaim their direction, and build the best years of their lives.

Not through affirmations, manifesting or vision boards, but by putting in the work and taking bold action.

If that resonates, book a free 30-minute intro call with me.

No pressure. No hard sell. If it's a fit, great. If not, you'll leave with more clarity and a few practical next steps.
